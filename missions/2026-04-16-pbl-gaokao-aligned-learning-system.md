## Goal

Design a comprehensive Project-Based Learning (PBL) system for ages 2-18 that:
1. Fully covers and aligns with China's 体制内高考体系 (national curriculum + college entrance exam)
2. Preserves and cultivates intrinsic motivation throughout all stages
3. Prepares students for the AI era by building capabilities that remain uniquely human
4. Transforms every required test/exam topic into daily real-world problems or frontier human problems
5. Builds 主体性 (agency) — the inner narrative of "我可以..." (I can investigate / build / figure this out)

The core mechanism: students never encounter "content to memorize." They encounter **problems worth solving**, and discover curriculum knowledge as the tools needed along the way.

## Scope

### Included
- Full PBL curriculum framework ages 2-18, divided into developmental stages
- Systematic mapping methodology: 高考 knowledge points → PBL problem sets
- Sample mappings for core subjects (Math, 语文, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, Politics)
- Motivation architecture across all stages
- 主体性 cultivation model with concrete practices per age
- AI integration philosophy (how AI tools are introduced and used at each stage)
- Assessment design (how to verify 高考 readiness without destroying PBL spirit)
- Risk analysis and mitigations vs. pure 应试 systems
- Parent/family integration model

### Excluded
- Detailed daily lesson plans (that's a later phase)
- Specific textbook selection
- School administration/operations
- Teacher training curriculum (later phase)
- Pricing/business model

## Notes

- The key insight: 高考 is not the enemy. The enemy is **decontextualized drilling**. The same knowledge, encountered through genuine problems, builds both exam readiness AND real capability.
- "我可以" is not a slogan — it's the measurable outcome. At every stage, students should be able to articulate what they investigated, built, or figured out, in their own words.
- AI is a force multiplier for PBL, not a threat. Students who learn *through* problems naturally learn to use AI as a tool, not a crutch.
- This system must be honest about the 高考 timeline: by age 16-18, exam preparation intensifies. The question is whether PBL foundations make that preparation more effective and less soul-crushing.
