# PBL-Gaokao System

**A Project-Based Learning framework for the full human lifespan, aligned with China's 高考 system, built for the AI era. Not just an education system — a system for agency, healing, and the curious life.**

## The Core Logic

One logic chain drives everything wrong with the current system:

> **No good scores → No good college → No good life → You die.**

This is the survivorship chain. It produces: anxiety, zero-sum competition, broken motivation, passive compliance, and generations of credentialed but hollow humans.

**The chain is a lie.** Not entirely — scores matter. The lie is that this is the ONLY path to a good life. The lie is that survival requires kneeling.

This project attacks the chain directly:

1. **Galaxy of Spirits** — A living collection of ordinary people who prove the chain is false. Evidence over theory.
2. **Agency (主体性)** — Replacing passive survival with chosen action. "I choose to survive AND create."
3. **Collaboration** — Replacing zero-sum competition with mutual strength. Together we create; alone we kneel.

## The Thesis

> Every 高考 knowledge point can be re-encountered as a real problem worth solving.
> The curriculum becomes the tool. The problem becomes the entry point.
> The student's inner narrative shifts from "我必须背这个" to **"我可以用这个解决..."**

But the thesis goes deeper: the adults must heal first. Every parent, teacher, and mentor carries their own survivorship wounds. The healing is prerequisite to the teaching. And the system extends beyond age 18 — the galaxy of problems is lifelong.

## Core Principles

1. **Full 高考 Coverage** — No knowledge point is skipped. Students following this system are exam-competitive.
2. **Problem-First Learning** — Every topic is introduced through a genuine problem, not an abstract lesson.
3. **主体性 (Agency)** — "我可以..." is the measurable outcome at every stage. Students own their learning.
4. **Motivation by Design** — Intrinsic drive is architecturally sustained, not left to chance.
5. **AI as Collaborator** — Students learn to work with AI tools as amplifiers, not replacements.
6. **Honest About Constraints** — The 高考 timeline is real. This system makes exam prep more effective, not optional.

## Age Stages

| Stage | Ages | Name | Core Mode |
|-------|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | 2-5 | Seedling | Sensory exploration, play-based inquiry, language immersion |
| 2 | 6-9 | Explorer | Structured curiosity, first projects, foundational skills through problems |
| 3 | 10-12 | Builder | Multi-week projects, cross-subject integration, growing independence |
| 4 | 13-15 | Investigator | Research-driven projects, 中考 alignment, identity formation |
| 5 | 16-18 | Architect | Capstone projects, 高考 synthesis, AI-augmented deep work |
| 6 | ~25-40 | Rediscoverer | Recovering from education. The galaxy went dark — now finding it again |
| 7 | ~35-55 | Builder-of-Systems | Designing systems that help OTHERS solve problems. The generative turn |
| 8 | ~50-70 | Sage-Explorer | Deep expertise + beginner's mind. Mentoring without imposing |
| 9 | ~65+ | Elder-Witness | Pattern recognition across decades. Teaching through story |

Stages 1-5 are the education system. Stages 6-9 extend the galaxy to the full human lifespan. The 2-year-old and the 90-year-old explore the same galaxy as co-pilots.

## The Key Mechanism: Test → Problem Transformation

Instead of:
> "Today we learn quadratic equations. Open your textbook to page 47."

We have:
> "A local farmer wants to maximize the area of a rectangular pen using 40 meters of fencing against a wall. What dimensions should she use? Let's figure it out."

The student discovers quadratic optimization because they need it, not because it's on the syllabus. But it IS on the syllabus — and now they understand it deeply.

This transformation is applied **systematically** to every required knowledge point across all subjects.

## The AI Ecosystem Vision

The resource analysis revealed: AI can handle 60-80% of every PBL resource requirement (teacher training, problem design, assessment, parent communication). The remaining 20-40% is the irreducible human core: safe environments, real relationships, genuine conviction.

This means you don't need to fight the system — you augment it. Pick a safe school, find good mentors, care about real interactions. AI handles the rest.

The vision: an open-source AI ecosystem — parent coaching, teacher self-training, problem design, red flag detection, exam optimization — free for every school, every family, every village. See `ai-ecosystem-vision.md`.
